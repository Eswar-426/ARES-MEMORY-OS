## 3. Engineering Decisions Validated

* **Non-Mutating Memory:** Evolution events (`EvolutionEvent`) and Drift (`DriftCandidate`) are decoupled from `GraphNode` semantics. Soft deletion (`deleted_at`) operates independently from evolution history.
* **Evidence Before Drift:** The architecture forbids arbitrary AI opinions from declaring drift. Facts from the Scanner/Evidence engine are mandatory prerequisites.
* **Deterministic Decay:** Fixed staleness thresholds were rejected in favor of a dynamic `Age × Volatility × Dependency Weight` model, providing a realistic assessment of memory health.
* **Traceability Integration:** Evolution works seamlessly with the `ares-traceability` engine, relying on canonical IDs and project scopes.
